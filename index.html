About

Hi, my name is Carl Hain and I am a web developer living in the Chicago suburbs. After many years working in marketing research and competitive intelligence for a large commercial insurance company, I decided to switch careers. I have always been passionate about technology and the web, and I have been a hobbyist programmer for many years; so the choice was obvious. I am especially excited by the MERN stack, which includes MongoDB, Express, React, and Node. I am also passionate about the new education opportunities that the web brings and would not have been able to do this without the help of Codecademy, Udacity, FreeCodeCamp, and so many other resources.
